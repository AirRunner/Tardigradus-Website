Chicky Graphs


What is this game ?

This game is the result of our collaboration with Chloé Crayon and Gabriela De Almeida during the third semester of our studies at Efrei Paris. We decided to go further than lists and trees in C language, so we developpeda gme based on graphs.

What is the theory of graphs? It covers everything about graphs: how to deal with them, their implementation, traversals graphs, spanning forests, etc...


What is the goal ?

Our idea was to develop a learning software dedicated to learning how graphs work and how to use them. A tree is a particular graph, thus it is a way for us to approach this notion and transmit what we learned through the different stages of creation of this project.

The public aimed is students like we were and even younger, aged from 15-16. Indeed, we wanted Chicky Graphs to be a fun way to approach a the complicated subject of the theory of graphs.


How to play ?

Function Command
Next Mission Next
Create Chick Left click
Delete Chick Right click
Move Chick Keep left click
Create link Keep right click between two Chicks
Delete link Keep left click between two Chicks
Infect Chick Press 'I' on the chick
Contaminate Chicks Press 'C'
Change texture of Chick press 'N' on the chick
